About Me

My career began with a curiosity and interest in coding, evolving into an inspired pursuit of excellency in website development. I am committed to staying abreast of industry trends, such as progressive web apps and AI-driven design, to deliver innovative solutions. My portfolio includes custom websites for small personal projects, reflecting my dedication to craftsmanship in every line of code.
